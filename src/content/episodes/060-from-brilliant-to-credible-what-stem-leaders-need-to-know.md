## Show Notes

This episode focuses on the gap between technical capability and external credibility.

Armando and Shayna break down the leadership signals that shape trust in the market, why many technical leaders underinvest in messaging, and how clearer communication affects fundraising, hiring, and customer confidence.

## Topics Covered

- The leadership signals visible leaders consistently project
- Why weak profile/messaging positioning creates business drag
- How credibility impacts fundraising, retention, and market trust
- Practical ways technical leaders can improve communication clarity
- What founders should be able to explain in under 30 seconds
