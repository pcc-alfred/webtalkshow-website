## Show Notes

This episode breaks down the gap between public assumptions and real groundwater constraints.

Armando covers how groundwater rights differ from surface water rights, why aquifer depletion is often invisible until it is too late, and how drought cycles create long-term planning pressure for homes, agriculture, and local businesses.

## Topics Covered

- The legal and operational reality of groundwater rights
- Why aquifer health matters even when water still comes out of the tap
- The business and community impact of prolonged drought
- What to monitor now to avoid expensive surprises later
