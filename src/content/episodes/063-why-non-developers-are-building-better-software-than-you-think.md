## Show Notes

Most people think software development still requires a developer. Jose Duarte is building iOS apps, Meta API integrations, and multi-agent pipelines without a computer science background, and he's doing it faster than most engineering teams would. Jose Duarte is a performance marketer and the head of growth at Pangea Money Transfer, with 15 years of experience in finance, mobile, and editorial. In this conversation, you will learn: - How Jose built a custom multi-agent workflow in Claude Code that mirrors a real software development lifecycle, from requirements to QA - Why he uses a \"virtual meeting\" plugin to plan features before any code gets written, and how it prevents the AI from going rogue on his codebase - The role that metaphors and analogies play in getting better, more accurate results from LLMs - How tools like Ralph (the Anthropic plugin) use bash loops to run autonomously until tests pass, without requiring you to intervene - Why the cost of starting over with AI is nearly zero, and how that changes the way you should approach building - What Jose discovered when he gave Claude Cowork access to his Gmail to plan a two-week trip to Japan - His theory on why AI will make human creativity more important, not less If you are building with AI tools or thinking about it, subscribe so you do not miss episodes like this one.
